Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Nairobi Chamber of Commerce

The name reflects the local business community in Nairobi and aims to provide business networking and economic development resources.

Site Purpose

  • To promote local businesses and economic growth by providing networking opportunities.
  • To serve as an informational hub for businesses, investors, and residents interested in the local economy.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming networking events are hosted by the Chamber?
  • Where can I find business registration and investment opportunities?
  • How can I become a member of the Chamber?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color (Dark Blue - #004080): Used for headings and navigation.
  • Secondary Color (Light Gray - #f4f4f4): Background color for improved readability.

Typography

  • Headings: "Montserrat", sans-serif.
  • Body Text: "Arial", sans-serif.
